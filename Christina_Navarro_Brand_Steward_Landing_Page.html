The Letter

I could begin with a list of my qualifications.
Instead, I'd like to share something
precious to me.

A small, sunny, yellow dandelion and its fuzzy, white seeds.

"That's not a flower. That's a weed."

No less, the dandelion remained a favorite of mine as a kid. Not because I preferred its look or taste — hey, curiosity isn't perfect — but because it promises, as it dies, a wish. Windblown, wispy dandelion seeds carry wishes and ideas in hopes of being planted.

Like dandelion seeds need the wind, Casago needs team members who are passionate about Owner-Centric™ property management, and advocating for homeowners and their properties above all. I'd love to apply my passion for hospitality, and my skills in crafting compelling messaging and content, to help share Casago's story and make an impact.

Throughout the years I've spent growing in marketing and project management at Vacasa, and in SaaS, hospitality, and customer service — I have built real expertise in being agile, scrappy, and a self-starter. Juggling multiple responsibilities in fast-paced environments, I consistently meet strict deadlines and thrive when coming up with creative solutions to hard problems.

Creativity and empathy are my strongest traits. I'm driven toward helping others, collaborating with teams, optimizing processes, and seeing every stage of an initiative through. My diligent attention to detail is consistent in everything I do. If there's ever a question I don't have the answer to, I find out, then document it as a resource for others.

As a first-generation American-born citizen who has lived in Spain, I write and speak Spanish fluently. Forever a student, I am always curious about new trends, digitally savvy, and committed to personalized care. Even with an ever-evolving digital marketing and STR landscape, Casago's credo can continue to thrive and spread — hopefully with my help, my dandelion seed.
